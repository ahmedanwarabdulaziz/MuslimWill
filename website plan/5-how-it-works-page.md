# Muslim Will How It Works Page

## Page Role

The How It Works page should make the Muslim Will process feel guided, professional, human, and carefully managed.

Its job is to:

- explain the full customer journey clearly
- show that Muslim Will is not a DIY template generator
- make the scholar review and Ontario legal workflow feel real and human
- explain final Zoom attestation safely and confidently
- reduce anxiety around what happens after payment
- support both individual and couples customers
- move users toward `Start Your Will`

This page should feel like a calm operating manual for families, not an internal workflow document.

## Strategic Decision

Use an 8-step public process.

The homepage can keep a shorter 4-step process. The How It Works page should show more detail because users who click this page are asking for reassurance before they start.
