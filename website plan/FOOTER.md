# Muslim Will Footer

## Footer Goal

The footer should feel:
- credible
- complete
- premium
- calm
- legally responsible

Its job is to:
- close the page cleanly
- give users a second navigation layer
- reinforce trust and clarity
- surface compliance, bios, and contact paths

## Design Direction

### Look And Feel
- deep green background
- warm cream or soft white text
- gold used sparingly for highlights and dividers
- spacious columns
- clean legal and contact details

### Recommended Colors
- background: `#004926`
- heading or accent: `#d4af37`
- body text: warm off-white

## Footer Trust Statement

### Recommended Short Line
`A will is a trust we fulfill before Allah, and a mercy we leave for our families after us.`

Use this as a soft emotional anchor near the brand column or above the lower legal row.

## Footer Disclaimer

### Recommended Disclaimer Copy
`Muslim Will is a structured planning platform and workflow system. It is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice or religious rulings. Legal validity depends on accurate information, proper review, and correct signing and witnessing under applicable law.`

This should appear in smaller text near the bottom, but still readable.

## Lower Footer Row

### Recommended Items
- `© 2026 Muslim Will. All rights reserved.`
- `Privacy Policy`
- `Terms of Service`
- `Disclaimer`

Optional:
- `Canada-first service with Ontario as the clearest current legal workflow.`

Use only if it fits cleanly and does not clutter the footer.

## Contact Notes

If contact details are displayed, keep them minimal and professional:
- `info@themuslimwill.com`
- `+1-877-416-WILL`

Do not overload with:
- too many social icons
- multiple addresses unless required
- cluttered badges or seals

## Social Links

Recommendation:
- include only if the brand actually maintains them well
- keep them small and understated

Suggested:
- Instagram
- Facebook
- LinkedIn if a professional presence exists

If social is weak or inactive, omit it.

## SEO Notes

The footer should support internal linking to:
- core service pages
- FAQ
- compliance page
- expert bios
- charitable legacy page

Avoid:
- large spammy link dumps
- every possible future page

## Mobile Footer

### Recommendation
- stack columns cleanly
- keep spacing generous
- keep CTAs visible but not oversized
- place disclaimer below the nav links
- make tap targets easy

## Recommended Final Footer Layout

### Top Footer Area
- column 1: brand and short description
- column 2: explore links
- column 3: experts and trust links
- column 4: action and contact

### Bottom Footer Area
- short trust/disclaimer line
- copyright
- legal links

## Professional Tone Check

The footer should feel:
- finished
- credible
- thoughtful
- structured
- not generic
