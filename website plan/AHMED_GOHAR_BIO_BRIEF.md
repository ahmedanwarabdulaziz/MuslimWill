# Ahmed Gohar Bio Brief

## Working Role
- Founder & CEO, Patriva / The Muslim Will

## Core Positioning
- technology founder solving the gap between generic wills and Shariah-conscious estate planning for Muslim families in the West
- builder of systems, not a lawyer or scholar figure
- focused on accessibility, scale, and professional structure

## Key Facts To Use
- founded The Muslim Will to address the absence of wills that are both Shariah-conscious and legally enforceable
- built The Muslim Will as a structured document generation platform
- positions the platform as a technology system, not a law firm and not a legal advisory service
- designed the workflow around four layers:
  - user input
  - scholar-validated inheritance logic
  - independent legal review
  - guided execution oversight
- founder of The Agile Group
- has experience building SaaS platforms, mobile applications, and regulated workflows
- has led products in complex and operationally sensitive sectors, including the medical sector
- known for building systems that combine software, operational process, and third-party integrations into products that actually ship and scale
- aims to standardize Shariah-conscious estate planning across the West and make it accessible beyond high-resource families

## Messaging Angles

### Founder Angle
- Ahmed is the system builder behind Muslim Will
- his contribution is the infrastructure that holds the pathway together responsibly

### Product Angle
- the platform is distinctive because each layer has a clear role
- no single layer overreaches into another

### Mission Angle
- Muslim families should not have to choose between a generic legal will and no will at all
- estate planning should be structured, accessible, and faith-conscious

## Language To Avoid
- do not present Ahmed as a licensed lawyer unless confirmed
- do not present him as issuing fatwas or religious rulings
- do not blur his founder role with legal or scholarly authority

## Strong Bio Lines
- `He does not just architect ideas; he builds infrastructure.`
- `The Muslim Will is the convergence of technical capability and a real community need.`
- `His goal is to make Shariah-conscious estate planning accessible at scale.`
