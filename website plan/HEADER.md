# Muslim Will Header

## Header Goal

The header should feel:
- premium
- calm
- trustworthy
- uncluttered
- conversion-aware

Its job is to:
- anchor the brand quickly
- help users reach key pages fast
- keep the primary CTA visible
- avoid looking like a template navbar

## Design Direction

### Look And Feel
- slim, elegant, and well-spaced
- soft glass or warm cream background on scroll
- strong typography hierarchy
- minimal borders
- subtle shadow only when needed

### Brand Colors
- background: `#eeeedd` or translucent warm cream
- text: deep green `#004926`
- accent: gold `#d4af37`

### Recommended Behavior
- transparent or very soft on top of hero
- becomes solid and slightly elevated on scroll
- sticky header on desktop and mobile

## Logo Area

### Left Side
- Muslim Will logo
- optional short wordmark

### Recommendation
Keep the logo area simple and authoritative.

Do not add:
- too many badges
- country flags
- long tagline inside the header

## Desktop Navigation

### Recommended Nav Items
- `How It Works`
- `Charitable Legacy`
- `FAQ`
- `For Charities`
- `About`

### About Dropdown
Recommended dropdown items:
- `About Muslim Will`
- `Ahmed Gohar`
- `Dr. Yasser Aboutaha`
- `Legal & Islamic Compliance`

### Why This Structure
- keeps the top level clean
- surfaces the most commercially important pages
- allows authority and compliance pages to remain easy to reach

## Header CTAs

### Primary CTA
`Start Your Will`

### Secondary Option
`Book a Call`

### Recommendation
Only one button should appear as the strong header CTA.

Use:
- primary visible button: `Start Your Will`
- `Book a Call` as a lighter text link or mobile menu item

This keeps the header from looking crowded and keeps the product-led path clear.

## Suggested Header Copy

### Logo Label
`Muslim Will`

### Optional Utility Microcopy
If needed in a very small top line or announcement bar:

`For Muslim families in Canada, with Ontario as the clearest current legal workflow.`

Use this only if it is visually elegant.
Do not force it into the main navigation row.

## Mobile Header

### Recommended Structure
- logo left
- menu button right
- CTA can remain inside the menu drawer or as a small visible button if space allows

### Mobile Menu Order
1. Start Your Will
2. How It Works
3. Charitable Legacy
4. FAQ
5. For Charities
6. About Muslim Will
7. Ahmed Gohar
8. Dr. Yasser Aboutaha
9. Legal & Islamic Compliance
10. Book a Call

### Mobile Style Notes
- full-height slide panel or elegant dropdown sheet
- generous spacing
- clear typography
- no cramped hamburger menu list

## UX Notes

- the header should not try to sell everything
- avoid too many nav items at the top level
- keep the CTA visible but not aggressive
- the first impression should be premium service, not SaaS dashboard

## Accessibility Notes

- strong contrast for text and buttons
- clear focus states
- descriptive `aria-label` on menu button
- visible active page state

## SEO And Internal Linking Notes

The header should prioritize internal links that matter most:
- core conversion pages
- FAQ
- charitable legacy
- authority pages

Do not overload the header with every future page.

## Recommended Final Header Layout

### Desktop
- left: logo
- center: navigation
- right: `Book a Call` text link + `Start Your Will` button

### Mobile
- left: logo
- right: menu icon
- inside drawer: all key links with `Start Your Will` pinned at the top

## Professional Tone Check

The header should feel:
- intentional
- restrained
- modern
- premium
- easy to trust
